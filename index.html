Our Privacy Promise

We read it, extract it, delete it

Your emails are private. SchoolSphere never stores the content of your emails or documents — only the events extracted from them.

📧

Emails are never stored

When you forward an email or connect Gmail, we read the content in memory, extract the event details, and immediately discard the email. We never save your email text.

📄

Documents deleted immediately

PDFs and photos you upload are processed by AI and permanently deleted the moment extraction is complete. No copies are kept anywhere.

🔒

UK GDPR compliant

SchoolSphere is operated under UK law. You have full rights to access, export, and delete all your data at any time from within the app.

🚫

Zero advertising. Zero selling.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the SchoolSphere service. We never sell your data.

FAQ

Questions parents ask before downloading

Is SchoolSphere free?

Yes — SchoolSphere is completely free during the beta period. We'll introduce optional paid plans in the future, but beta users will always get a generous free tier.

Does it read all my emails?

No. If you connect Gmail or Outlook, SchoolSphere only reads emails from senders you've added to your trusted senders list. It never touches personal or unrelated emails.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Email content is never stored — it's read, extracted, and discarded immediately. Uploaded documents are deleted the moment processing is complete. We're UK GDPR compliant.

Do I need to install an app?

No app store needed. SchoolSphere is a Progressive Web App — just open schoolsphere.app in your browser and tap "Add to Home Screen" for a native app experience.

Can my partner use it too?

Yes! Use Family Sharing to invite a partner, grandparent, or nanny. You control which children and events they can see.

What if the AI gets the date wrong?

Every extracted event is shown to you for review before it's added. You can edit any detail — date, time, title, requirements — before confirming.

Does it work with Google Calendar?

You can export any event as an .ics file to add to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook. Native two-way sync is on our roadmap.

What schools does it work with?

Any school — SchoolSphere works with any email or document from any school. It's not connected to any school system; it simply reads what you share with it.
